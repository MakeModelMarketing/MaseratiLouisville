Maserati GranTurismo Convertible Interior Color Options

The GranTurismo Convertible is a new class of top-down driving. Every detail, inside and out, is meticulously crafted with care. From the leathers seats and stitching to the carpet and dashboard, the GranTurismo Convertible offers a plethora of customization options to meet your personal preference. In total, the GranTurismo Convertible offers 10 interior leather seat color options that include:

GranTurismo Convertible Interior Equipment

Designing your dream convertible doesn't stop at the leather upholstery. The GranTurismo Convertible offers additional equipment to spruce up your ride. Options include aluminium pedals, steering wheel shift paddles, remote control to open garage (Homelink) and Bose Premium Sound System. To reach the highest level of interior, we recommend the Dual-color interior option. This features front door panels and rear quarter panels with dual-color layout, so you can perfectly match the color of the dashboard and rear shelf.
