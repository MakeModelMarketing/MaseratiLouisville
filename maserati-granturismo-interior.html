Maserati GranTurismo Interior Color Options

There’s no doubt the GranTurismo is a masterpiece of Italian design. With the array of interior leather and Alcantara fabric options, you can take Maserati’s design concept and make it your own. Whether your taste is luxury or sport, you’ll have no problem personalizing the GranTurismo. In total, the GranTurismo offers 10 interior leather seat color options, suiting a variety of styles:

GranTurismo Interior Equipment

You can express your style even further by adding more interior equipment to your GranTurismo. These features include Bose Premium Sound System, Remote Control to Open Garage (Homelink), Pedals with Black Cover, Aluminium Pedals, Door Sill Covers and Drilled Leather for Central Seats Zone. To reach the highest level of luxury design, we recommend the Dual-color interior option. This features front door panels and rear quarter panels with dual-color layout, so it can perfectly match with the color of dashboard and rear shelf.
